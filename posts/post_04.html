One anthem, two brothers

...one English, one Scottish

Posted by Frankie Shattock on June 28, 2023

There's been a bit of a falling out between me and my brother Nobby regarding our football anthem "Think it's all over?". To get straight to the point, Nobby is English. I'm Scottish (don't be fooled by the accent, I hail from Dingwall). Nobby's veins run red, white and blue. He wanted the song to be and England anthem and that was the end of it.

Nobby's England Mix

"Well stuff that!" I says. And I put my foot down. Refused to set foot in the studio. We'd reach an effin' impasse.

Then in steps our producer, Menzies Farqhuar. And did the whole King Solomon "...let's cut the baby in two shall we lads?" routine.

And that what we did. We've each got half a baby to call our own. Two different mixes of the same bleeding song. Take a listen and let me know on twitter which one is best (Spoiler: my one)

Frankie's Scotland Mix

If you're listening Nobby? UP YOURS!
